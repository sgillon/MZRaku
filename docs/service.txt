Cleaning of head
The head is critical for a proper performance of the tape
recorder. Dust on the head, capstan, pinch roller, etc.
impedes proper recording and playback. Open the cassette
holder, take out the tape, push down the PLAYBACK
pushbutton, then clean those components. If you can
see any oxide deposit, clean them using a cotton bud
damped with alcohol.

depression of the button may result in machine failure.
